What materials are used in your decorative products?

Our decorative products are crafted from a variety of materials, including wood, metal, glass, ceramic, resin, and fabric. Each material is chosen for its durability and aesthetic appeal.

How should I clean and maintain my decorative items?

The cleaning and maintenance instructions vary depending on the material of the product. Generally, a soft, damp cloth can be used for dusting. For specific materials, please refer to the product's care instructions provided on the packaging or product page.

Do you offer customization or personalization of decorative products?

Yes, we offer customization and personalization services for certain products. You can add a personal touch by customizing items with names, dates, or specific designs. Please check the product page for customization options.

How do I hang or install wall decor items?

Most of our wall decor items come with hanging hardware or instructions for installation. We recommend using appropriate wall anchors and screws for secure installation. If you have any specific questions, please contact our customer service.

Are your decorative products handmade?

Many of our decorative items are handcrafted by skilled artisans, which makes each piece unique. Look for the "handmade" label on the product page for more information.

Can I get a discount if I buy in bulk?

We offer discounts on bulk purchases for many of our products. For large orders, please contact our sales team to discuss pricing and availability.
